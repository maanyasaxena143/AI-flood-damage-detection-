# 🌊 AI-Powered Flood Damage Detection System

## Overview

Floods are among the most devastating natural disasters, causing significant damage to infrastructure, property, and human lives. Manual damage assessment is often time-consuming, resource-intensive, and difficult to perform at scale during emergency situations.

This project presents an AI-powered Flood Damage Detection System that leverages Deep Learning and Computer Vision techniques to automatically analyze flood-affected images and classify damage levels. The solution aims to support disaster response teams, government agencies, insurance companies, and relief organizations by enabling faster and more efficient damage assessment.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional flood damage assessment relies heavily on manual inspection, which can delay rescue operations, relief distribution, and recovery planning.

The objective of this project is to:

- Automate damage assessment using AI.
- Reduce human effort and inspection time.
- Improve decision-making during disaster response.
- Provide scalable image-based damage analysis.
